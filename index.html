About the practice

A grounded, relational therapeutic approach

Claudelle provides a client centred and relational approach that promotes resilience and respects self determination. Sessions focus on understanding what is happening for you, strengthening coping skills, and supporting meaningful change at a pace that feels safe and practical.

Experience includes supporting people across diverse community backgrounds and a broad range of social, personal, and mental health concerns, including work in education settings (University and TAFE contexts).

Evidence based practice supported by ongoing professional development.

"Calm, practical support that helped me make sense of what I was dealing with."
— Client feedback

Evidence based modalities

Claudelle draws from established therapeutic frameworks. The methods used are selected based on your needs, preferences, and goals.

Trauma focused therapy Strength based therapy Motivational interviewing Narrative therapy Attachment theory Cognitive behaviour therapy (CBT) Solution focused therapy Psychodynamic therapy Feminist theories of change Psycho education

You do not need to know what approach you need. We can work it out together.
